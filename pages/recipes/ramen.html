Easy recipe to make homemade Ramen

Newbie chefs put dry ramen in a bowl and pour water over it, but the trick is to actually boil the ramen in the water for a couple of minutes.

Is ramen just a salty cereal?

Steps

  • Put water in a deep pan or pot
  • After it starts to boil, put ramen in the water and boil them for 5 minutes
  • Pour almostall of the water out. Dont full dry the ramen, leave a little "puddle" on the bottom so it can absorb and mix with the sauce

Ingredients

  1. Ramen
  2. Water

This is a recipe to make ramen, not ramen with stuff
