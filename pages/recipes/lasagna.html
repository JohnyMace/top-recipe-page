Lasagna


Easy recipe to make homemade lasagna

Is lasagna a sandwich?

Hear me out here. In sandwich, you put bread layers and fill the between space with nonbread items. Pasta is made from dough and you fill the between spaces with meat and sauce!

Also ocean is a soup. Huge container of water with all kinds of vegetables and meat in it.

Steps

  • Boil the pasta slabs
  • Cook the ground beef
  • Cook bechamel sauce
  • Stacc pasta slab and meat in turns until you fill out your lasagna container
  • Cover all of the top layer with bechamel sauce and ground parmesan cheese
  • Bake for X amount of degrees for Y amount of time

Ingredients

  1. Lasagna pasta
  2. Ground meat of your preference
  3. Ingredients for bechamel sauce
  4. Žán Parmažán cheese, ideally pre ground so you don't have to clean the grinder
