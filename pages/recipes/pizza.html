Pizza


Easy recipe to make homemade Pizza

Does pineaple belong on the pizza? This single question is preventing us from achieving world peace

Wait until you hear about the nutella sweet pizza

Steps

  • Make a dough. Flour, water, oil eggs you can do it
  • Make a circle from the dough
  • Spread a tomato sauce all over it, not too thicc, You decide if you want to buy a finished sauce or look up a tomato sauce recipe elsewhere
  • Choose deli meat of your liking - ham, salami, any meat in slice form will do and put a generous layer. Make sure to still be able to see the sauce under all the meat
  • Get a big, ripe juicy pineaple. Remove all the pineapple armor. Now that you have the pineaple core, lay it on it's side and chop it into circles as thicc as you want your pineaple pieces to be. Now that your pineaple is in circles, you can easily cut it inco cubes
  • Put all of the pineaple cubes in a bowl and throw it in the trashbin
  • Put your pizza, untainted, uncorrupted with pineaple, into an oven and bake at x amounf of degrees for y amount of time

Ingredients

  1. Dough
  2. Tomato sauce
  3. Slices of deli meat of your liking
  4. Pineaple (one more pineaple you manage to kill and throw out, one less hawaii pizza)
